# Operations System Case Study

> Designing operational systems that connect people, processes, and technology.

This repository demonstrates how I approach operational design by transforming complex, manual business processes into scalable, repeatable systems.

All examples use generalized workflows and sample data. No proprietary or confidential information is included.

---

# Business Challenge

Organizations often manage critical operations through disconnected:

- Emails
- Spreadsheets
- Forms
- Documents
- Manual approvals
- Individual knowledge

As organizations grow, these disconnected processes become harder to manage, resulting in duplicated work, inconsistent reporting, communication gaps, and limited visibility.

---

# My Approach

I start with the business problem—not the software.

Every solution follows the same principles:

✔ Understand the workflow

✔ Standardize the process

✔ Centralize information

✔ Automate repetitive work

✔ Improve reporting

✔ Support better decision making

Technology is the tool.

Operations are the product.
