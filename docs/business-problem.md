# Business Problem

## Scenario

A growing professional association hosts multiple annual conferences.

Operational data is managed through:

- Email
- Registration platform
- Speaker spreadsheets
- Sponsor spreadsheets
- Financial tracking
- Manual reporting

## Challenges

- Duplicate information
- Manual reporting
- Communication delays
- Limited visibility
- Multiple data sources
- No centralized operational dashboard

## Goal

Design a scalable operational system that centralizes information, automates repetitive tasks, and improves executive visibility while reducing administrative effort.
