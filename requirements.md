# Business Requirements

## Purpose

The purpose of this project is to demonstrate how a centralized operational system can replace fragmented manual processes used to manage conferences and events.

---

## Functional Requirements

The system should support:

- Sponsor management
- Speaker management
- Attendee management
- Session management
- Workflow automation
- Status tracking
- Executive reporting
- Operational dashboards

---

## Operational Requirements

The system should:

- Reduce manual data entry
- Standardize operational workflows
- Centralize operational data
- Improve reporting accuracy
- Increase visibility across departments
- Support organizational growth

---

## Success Criteria

A successful implementation should result in:

- Fewer manual processes
- Faster reporting
- Better operational visibility
- Improved communication
- Consistent workflow execution
- Better executive decision making
